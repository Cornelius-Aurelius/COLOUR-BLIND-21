Blackjack 21 Rules

  • Goal: get closer to 21 than the dealer without going over.
  • Player gets 2 cards. Dealer gets 2 cards.
  • One dealer card is hidden until the player stands.
  • 2–10 count as face value.
  • Jack, Queen and King count as 10.
  • Ace counts as 1 or 11, whichever helps the hand more.
  • Blackjack is Ace + 10-value card on the first 2 cards.
  • Blackjack pays 3:2.
  • Dealer hits on 16 or less.
  • Dealer stands on 17 or more.
  • Dealer stands on Soft 17.
  • Same score is a push and the bet is returned.

Colour-Blind Football Training

The cards are football kits. The aim is to train attention to non-colour cues: shirt number, pattern, sleeve shape, socks, shorts, contrast and badge position.
