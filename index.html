How To Play

  • STAND = dealer kit cards match.
  • HIT = dealer kit cards do not match.
  • Correct answer = player receives the kit card value.
  • Wrong answer = dealer wins.
  • Land exactly on 21 = player wins.
  • Go over 21 = dealer wins.

Training Focus

Check shirt number, pattern, sleeves, socks, shorts, badge position, collar, shape and contrast. Do not rely on colour alone.
