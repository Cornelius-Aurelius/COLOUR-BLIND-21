Colour-Blindness Training Bonus

If your winning hand contains two exact matching football kits, your profit is doubled. This rewards careful recognition of non-colour cues such as shirt number, pattern, badge, socks, shorts and collar.

Blackjack Rules

  • Goal: get closer to 21 than the dealer without going over.
  • Player gets 2 cards. Dealer gets 2 cards.
  • One dealer card is hidden until the player stands.
  • 2–10 count as face value.
  • Jack, Queen and King count as 10.
  • Ace counts as 1 or 11.
  • Blackjack pays 3:2.
  • Dealer hits on 16 or less.
  • Dealer stands on 17 or more, including Soft 17.
  • Same score is a push and the bet is returned.
