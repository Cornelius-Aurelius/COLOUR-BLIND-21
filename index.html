How It Trains The Eye

This game changes Blackjack into a football kit-recognition challenge. The player must look past colour and compare practical football cues.

  • Shirt number
  • Pattern and stripes
  • Socks and shorts
  • Badge position
  • Collar and sleeve cues
  • Light/dark contrast

Rules

Correct answer gets the card value. Wrong answer means dealer wins. Land exactly on 21 to win the round. Go over 21 and the dealer wins.

How To Play

This is a football-specific Blackjack 21 game. The cards are football kits. Your job is to decide whether the two kit cards are identical.

  • STAND = the kits match.
  • HIT = the kits do not match.
  • Correct answer = you receive the card value.
  • Land exactly on 21 = you win the round and receive a blackjack bonus.
  • Go over 21 = dealer wins.
  • Wrong answer = dealer wins.
  • Choose different chip amounts before each decision.
  • Train non-colour cues: number, pattern, socks, sleeves, shorts, badge, collar, shape and contrast.
